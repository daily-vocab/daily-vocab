about daily-vocab

Hey! Welcome to daily-vocab. Created (still a work in progress though) to help with my French Learning Journey. The basic idea is this - create a place where you can add new words you're learning in your target language, along with how you can use it in a sentence; and maybe some notes as well.

Once you've added a certain amount of words, the app can present you with game (a matching sort of a game) to help you remember these words. This, improving your vocabulary.

The daily part comes in - to help you make this a daily habit by reminding you to add a new word everyday.

Would love to hear your thoughts - Saurav
